FAQ

Common Questions

Is this app for beginners or experienced athletes? ▼
Both. FORGEIQ uses the NASM Optimum Performance Training model to meet you exactly where you are. Beginners start with Phase 1 (Stabilization) and progress naturally. Advanced athletes can train in Phase 3-5 with sport-specific protocols. Your AI coach adapts to your level automatically.
How does the AI coach work? ▼
Your AI coach is powered by Claude (by Anthropic) and trained on the expertise of Dr. Michael Lovato and Debora Lovato. It knows your goals, your injuries, your medication status, and your fitness level. Every workout recommendation, nutrition suggestion, and coaching message is personalized to your specific situation. It is not a generic chatbot — it is a coach that remembers you.
Is FORGEIQ designed for GLP-1 users? ▼
Yes — FORGEIQ is one of the only fitness platforms specifically designed for people on GLP-1 medications like Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, and Zepbound. The AI coach prioritizes muscle preservation, adjusts protein targets to 1-1.2g per pound of bodyweight, manages fatigue-aware programming, and Debora provides skin and wellness support for post-weight-loss changes.
Can my partner and I use the app together? ▼
Absolutely. The Train Together mode gives each partner their own AI profile, their own programming based on their individual fitness level and goals, and shared challenges you can complete together. Dr. Michael and Debora built this from their own experience — fitness transformed their relationship, and they built FORGEIQ to do the same for others.
What makes FORGEIQ different from other fitness apps? ▼
Three things. First, it is built by coaches who have lived it — injury, weight loss, mental health challenges, and transformation. Second, the AI is trained on real NASM protocols, not generic fitness advice. Third, it addresses the dimensions other apps ignore: GLP-1 medication support, skin and body confidence (through Debora), veteran and mental health coaching, couples training, and injury-adaptive programming. FORGEIQ is not a workout generator — it is a coaching platform.
