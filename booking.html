Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need to be a FORGEIQ subscriber to book? ▼
No — coaching sessions are available to anyone. FORGEIQ app subscribers receive priority booking and a discount on all paid sessions.
Are sessions virtual or in person? ▼
All sessions are currently conducted virtually via Zoom or Google Meet. In-person sessions at Best Fitness are available by arrangement — ask during your discovery call.
What if I am on a GLP-1 medication — is there a specific session for me? ▼
Yes — the GLP-1 Fitness Consultation with Dr. Lovato is specifically designed for clients on semaglutide, tirzepatide, or similar medications. We cover muscle preservation, fatigue management, protein targets, and a progressive exercise protocol built around your medication schedule.
Can I book for both myself and my partner? ▼
The Couples Coaching Session is designed for exactly that — both of you on one call with both coaches. You each get a separate program and a shared challenge goal to work on together.
What is your cancellation policy? ▼
We ask for 24 hours notice for cancellations or reschedules. Life happens — just communicate and we will make it work. No-shows on paid sessions are non-refundable.
I have injuries — can you still help me? ▼
Absolutely. Dr. Lovato holds the NASM Corrective Exercise Specialist (CES) certification and has personal experience with torn shoulder, knee surgery, torn bicep, plantar fasciitis, and arthritis. We build around what you have — not what you had.
