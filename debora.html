Her Story

Debora has always been driven by one thing — helping the people around her become the best version of themselves. Not just physically, but in their confidence, their skin, and their sense of self.

She is passionate about fitness and self-confidence as a whole, supporting everyone around her, helping couples thrive together. Her approach combines the science of nutrition and exercise with the art of aesthetic wellness — because transformation is not just about the scale.

Together with her husband Dr. Michael Lovato, she built FORGEIQ on the belief that fitness transforms couples — and that when two people challenge themselves together, they discover strength they did not know existed.

As a licensed esthetician, laser technician, and NASM-certified nutrition and wellness coach, she addresses the full picture of transformation — including the dimensions every other fitness app ignores. Post-weight-loss skin laxity. GLP-1 aesthetic side effects. The confidence that comes from feeling at home in your body.
